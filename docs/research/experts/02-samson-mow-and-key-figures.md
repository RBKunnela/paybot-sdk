### 1.2 Bitcoin Bond (Volcano Bond) Initiative with El Salvador

The Volcano Bond was announced in November 2021 as a landmark $1 billion sovereign Bitcoin bond, designed and architected primarily by Samson Mow during his time at Blockstream.

**Bond structure:**
- 10-year maturity with a 6.5% coupon (roughly half the ~13% El Salvador's traditional bonds commanded)
- $500 million allocated to building Bitcoin City (a new city at the base of the Conchagua volcano, powered by geothermal energy, with zero taxes except VAT)
- $500 million allocated to purchasing Bitcoin, with capital gains after 5 years split 50/50 between the country and bondholders
- Issued as a tokenized security on Blockstream's Liquid Network, distributed by Bitfinex
- Mow estimated APY of 146% over the 10-year period, assuming Bitcoin reaches $1 million ([Bitcoin Magazine](https://bitcoinmagazine.com/markets/el-salvador-president-nayib-bukele-samson-mow-volcano-bitcoin-bond); [Nasdaq](https://www.nasdaq.com/articles/on-the-ground-in-el-salvador-with-samson-mow-and-the-volcano-bitcoin-bond))

**Investor interest:** Mow claimed $500 million in verbal commitments, primarily from crypto whales and related hedge funds, with institutional money expected later ([PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/blockchain/bitcoin/2022/blockstream-cso-500m-committed-to-el-salvador-bitcoin-bond/)).

**Current status (March 2026):** Despite years of advocacy, the Volcano Bond has never been officially launched. Mow has acknowledged this: "We haven't yet brought a country to do something significant. Even El Salvador never launched the Bitcoin bonds. But we are in negotiations with some countries to do Bitcoin bonds" ([Atlas21](https://atlas21.com/samson-mow-governments-will-want-bitcoin-because-its-valuable-just-as-they-did-with-gold/)). The IMF has continued to oppose El Salvador's Bitcoin strategy, warning that public debt could hit 96% of GDP in 2026 ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2022/03/14/el-salvador-president-bitcoin-city-volcano-bond-nayib-bukele/)).

## Part 2: Other Prominent Figures

### 2.1 Michael Saylor (Strategy, formerly MicroStrategy)

Michael Saylor is the Executive Chairman of Strategy (NASDAQ: MSTR), the company he co-founded in 1989 as a business intelligence firm before pivoting it into the world's largest corporate Bitcoin treasury in August 2020. As of late February 2026, Strategy holds **717,722 BTC** -- more than 3.4% of Bitcoin's total 21 million supply -- acquired for approximately $54.6 billion at an average price of ~$76,020 per bitcoin. At current prices (~$66-68K), the company sits on roughly $7.1 billion in unrealized losses. The stock has fallen approximately 71% from its summer 2025 peak ([The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/390770/if-its-not-going-to-zero-its-going-to-a-million-michael-saylors-strategy-buys-more-bitcoin); [The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/390010/michael-saylor-strategy-latest-bitcoin-acquisition)).

Strategy's acquisition pace has been relentless. In January 2026 alone, the company purchased 22,305 BTC for ~$2.1 billion in a single week -- its largest weekly haul since November 2024. Even as Bitcoin crashed through February, Saylor continued buying, acquiring smaller tranches of 855, 1,142, 2,486, and 592 BTC at progressively lower prices. He acknowledged the market is in a "crypto winter" but called it "much milder" and "shorter" than previous winters. His most quotable line from this period: "If it's not going to zero, it's going to a million" ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/02/09/michael-saylor-s-strategy-made-modest-bitcoin-acquisition-during-last-week-s-crypto-crash); [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/billionaire-michael-saylor-strategy-buys-131412932.html)).

Strategy's influence extends beyond its own balance sheet. According to Bitcoin Treasuries data, 194 public companies have adopted some form of Bitcoin acquisition model, many inspired by Saylor's playbook. Tokyo-listed Metaplanet has become the fourth-largest corporate holder with 35,102 BTC. Saylor has stated Strategy's capital structure can withstand a 90% Bitcoin price drop lasting 4-5 years, thanks to its mix of equity, convertible debt, and preferred instruments ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2025/12/15/michael-saylor-s-strategy-made-second-consecutive-usd1b-bitcoin-purchase-last-week); [Strategy](https://www.strategy.com/purchases)).

### 2.2 Cathie Wood (ARK Invest)

Cathie Wood, CEO of ARK Invest, remains one of Wall Street's most prominent Bitcoin bulls. ARK's Big Ideas 2026 report projects a 700% increase in Bitcoin's market capitalization over the next four years. Wood maintains a 2030 base case target of $700,000-$750,000 and a bull case of $1.2 million (revised down from $1.5 million in November 2025 due to stablecoin growth and gold's market performance). She has also reaffirmed a $1 million price target during podcast appearances, attributing confidence to stablecoin market capitalization surpassing $300 billion ([Daily Hodl](https://dailyhodl.com/2025/05/09/ark-invest-ceo-cathie-wood-doubles-down-on-bitcoin-prediction-says-base-case-target-for-btc-remains-700000/); [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/cathie-wood-confirms-1-million-104607784.html)).

Wood's central thesis is that Bitcoin's traditional four-year cycle is being "disrupted" by institutional participation. She argues that sharp 75-90% crashes from earlier eras are becoming less common as large financial players accumulate the asset: "The volatility's going down. Institutions are going to prevent much more of a decline." ARK's research shows U.S. spot Bitcoin ETFs and public companies now hold about 12% of total Bitcoin supply. The firm increased its digital gold total addressable market by 37% after gold's market cap rose 64.5% in 2025, while reducing expectations for Bitcoin's role as an emerging-market safe haven due to stablecoin adoption in developing economies. ARK continues to add crypto exposure through shares of Coinbase, Circle, and its own ARK 21Shares Bitcoin ETF (ARKB) ([Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/351790/ark-invests-cathie-wood-bitcoins-four-year-cycle-will-be-disrupted); [The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/386560/cathie-wood-ark-invest-bitcoin-crypto-2030)).

For 2026 specifically, Wood believes markets may be underestimating how different the year could look. She argues that the resilience shown by asset prices through tariff turmoil, a government shutdown, and hawkish Fed rhetoric in 2025 is "setting the stage for a potential Goldilocks year" where growth accelerates and inflation falls sharply ([TheStreet](https://www.thestreet.com/crypto/trading/cathie-wood-predicts-2026-revised-outlook)).

### 2.4 Lyn Alden

Lyn Alden is an independent macro analyst and investment strategist whose research has become essential reading for Bitcoin investors. Her central thesis is that Bitcoin is a "global liquidity barometer" -- her research shows Bitcoin moves in the direction of global M2 money supply 83% of the time over any 12-month period, making it the asset most correlated with global liquidity among all major asset classes ([Markets.com](https://www.markets.com/news/bitcoin-market-cycle-analysis-lyn-alden-2607-en); [CryptoSlate](https://cryptoslate.com/inside-the-mind-of-lyn-alden-bitcoin-ai-and-the-unstoppable-deficit-train/)).

Her "Nothing Stops This Train" thesis -- delivered as a keynote at Bitcoin 2025 -- argues that U.S. fiscal deficits are structurally unavoidable due to political polarization and mechanical debt levels. With U.S. debt above $38 trillion, she sees Bitcoin as a protective asset against currency debasement. On cycle dynamics, Alden believes the traditional four-year halving pattern is becoming less predictive, with institutional adoption, corporate treasury strategies, and political factors reshaping market cycles. She noted that current conditions "do not resemble past market peaks" and euphoric levels have not been reached in this cycle ([Lyn Alden](https://www.lynalden.com/bitcoin-stocks-and-bonds/); [Bitbo](https://bitbo.io/news/lynalden-bitcoin-capitulation-risk/)).

For 2025-2026, Alden expected Bitcoin to end 2025 higher than ~$85K (where it traded in April 2025) but cautioned that tariffs and geopolitical changes tempered her outlook. She anticipates domestic liquidity to remain "purposely rangebound" into late 2025 and early 2026, with the Fed's quantitative tightening slowly draining liquidity. A key catalyst would be a Fed pivot toward monetary easing, especially in response to a bond market crisis. She has cautioned investors that "no one is owed a bull market" ([Lyn Alden Premium](https://www.lynalden.com/premium-2025-6-8/); [CurrencyDates](https://currencydates.com/lynden-alden-bitcoin-2025-prediction-macro-trends-liquidity-outlook/)).

### 2.5 Balaji Srinivasan

Balaji Srinivasan, former CTO of Coinbase and General Partner at Andreessen Horowitz, is best known for his book *The Network State: How to Start a New Country* (2022), which argues that internet-native communities can evolve into sovereign political entities through shared values, integrated cryptocurrency, and globally distributed territory rather than traditional geographic conquest ([The Network State](https://thenetworkstate.com/)).

In 2025, Srinivasan moved from theory to practice by purchasing a private island near Singapore to serve as a physical campus for his "Network State" concept -- designed for startup founders, technologists, and fitness enthusiasts. He is reportedly planning additional Network School campuses in Dubai, Tokyo, and Miami. The concept has drawn praise from figures like Vitalik Buterin ("Now The Network State shows us how to start new cities and new countries") and Brian Armstrong ("Balaji was right"), though critics have raised concerns about provision of public goods and accused the movement of "fascist-chic cosplay" ([The Indian Eye](https://theindianeye.com/2025/07/07/balaji-srinivasan-carves-out-a-network-state-on-singapore-island/); [Noah Smith](https://www.noahpinion.blog/p/network-state-or-a-network-of-states); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2024/12/10/balaji-srinivasan-spreading-the-network-state)).

Srinivasan's crypto predictions have been characteristically bold. He predicted that "by the end of the 2020s, cryptocurrency becomes what all politics is about" and that governments will be unable to fund themselves without it. While his infamous $1M Bitcoin bet in 2023 (predicting BTC would hit $1M within 90 days) did not materialize, the Network State movement continues to gain momentum as a practical political experiment at the intersection of technology, governance, and cryptocurrency ([Tim Ferriss Blog](https://tim.blog/2022/07/04/balaji-srinivasan-network-state/)).

### 2.6 Vitalik Buterin (Ethereum)

Vitalik Buterin, co-founder of Ethereum, has been steering the network through one of its most significant strategic pivots in 2025-2026. In a major shift, Buterin declared in February 2026 that the original rollup-centric roadmap -- which positioned Layer 2s as Ethereum's primary scaling path -- "no longer makes sense." Two reasons: L2 decentralization progress has been slower than expected, and Ethereum itself is now scaling directly on Layer 1, with fees remaining low and gas limits expected to increase significantly ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/02/27/vitalik-buterin-reveals-his-bold-new-plan-to-fix-ethereum-s-scaling-problem); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/02/03/you-are-not-scaling-ethereum-vitalik-buterin-issues-a-blunt-reality-check-to-the-biggest-crypto-networks)).

His "Strawmap" -- published February 27, 2026 -- outlines how Ethereum could handle up to 1,000x its current transaction capacity while keeping node operation accessible. The plan covers three areas: execution (block-level access lists for parallel processing), data (separating storage costs from computation costs in gas pricing), and state (zkEVM integration). Near-term, the "Glamsterdam" and "ePBS" upgrades will improve block verification efficiency. By 2027, widespread zkEVM adoption could allow aggressive gas limit increases. Buterin also unveiled a quantum computing preparedness roadmap, proposing hash-based signatures and wallet flexibility (EIP-8141), shortly after the Ethereum Foundation established a dedicated post-quantum research team ([CoinGape](https://coingape.com/markets/ethereum-price-prediction-2026-as-vitalik-buterin-unveils-new-scaling-roadmap/); [NewsBTC](https://www.newsbtc.com/news/ethereum/vitalik-buterin-lays-out-a-plan-to-make-ethereum-1000-times-more-capable/); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/02/26/vitalik-buterin-unveils-ethereum-roadmap-to-counter-quantum-computing-threat)).

2025 achievements included gas limit increases from 30M to 36M units, blob capacity expanding from 3 to 6 (Pectra upgrade), and zkEVM proving times dropping from 16 minutes to 16 seconds -- a 60x improvement. Buterin emphasized the "walkaway test" (systems should run regardless of who maintains them) and stressed that Ethereum's mission is not about "chasing the next trend, whether that involves tokenized dollars, political meme coins, or efforts to fill block space for short-term objectives" ([CryptoPotato](https://cryptopotato.com/vitalik-buterin-ethereum-progressed-in-2025-must-decentralize-in-2026/); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/01/01/vitalik-buterin-on-the-two-goals-ethereum-must-meet-to-become-the-world-computer)).

### 2.7 Andreas Antonopoulos

Andreas M. Antonopoulos (born 1972, London) remains the Bitcoin community's most respected educator, despite being less active in headline-making commentary compared to more market-focused figures. A British-Greek early Bitcoin advocate, he is the author of bestselling technical books *Mastering Bitcoin*, *Mastering Ethereum*, and the *Internet of Money* series (volumes I-III). He has uploaded over 600 ad-free educational videos to YouTube and has delivered talks at universities, conferences, and parliaments worldwide ([Andreas Antonopoulos](https://aantonop.com/); [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Antonopoulos)).

Antonopoulos serves as a teaching fellow for the M.Sc. Digital Currencies program at the University of Nicosia and co-authored the curriculum for their free Introduction to Digital Currencies course. In March 2025, he led an open course on "New Opportunities for Venture Growth" at the Bucharest International School of Management. He participated in the 2025 Bitcoin Conference as a speaker. His educational philosophy centers on Bitcoin as a tool for financial freedom and censorship resistance rather than wealth accumulation. Critically, he maintains strict independence -- refusing to endorse altcoin projects for personal gain, declining advertising offers from questionable tokens, and remaining unaffiliated with exchanges, hedge funds, or corporate backers ([Blockchain Magazine](https://blockchainmagazine.com/leadership/andreas-antonopoulos-the-leading-voice-in-bitcoin-and-blockchain-education/); [Market Insiders](https://marketinsiders.in/2025/09/16/andreas-antonopoulos-bitcoin-evangelism/); [Bitcoin News](https://bitcoinnews.com/opinion/andreas-antonopoulos-bitcoin/)).

### 2.9 Elizabeth Stark (Lightning Labs)

Elizabeth Stark is co-founder and CEO of Lightning Labs, the company building the programmable financial layer of the internet on top of Bitcoin's Lightning Network. Under Stark's leadership, Lightning Labs has transformed the Lightning Network from a Bitcoin-only payment layer into a multi-asset network through **Taproot Assets** -- a protocol that enables assets like stablecoins to be minted on Bitcoin and sent over Lightning instantly for low fees ([Lightning Engineering](https://lightning.engineering/posts/2025-6-24-tapd-v0.6-launch/); [Lightning Engineering](https://lightning.engineering/posts/2025-12-16-tapd-0.7-launch/)).

Two major releases defined 2025: **Taproot Assets v0.6** (June 2025) launched multi-asset Lightning on mainnet, with Tether announcing USDT integration on Bitcoin via Taproot Assets. **Taproot Assets v0.7** (December 2025) added reusable addresses, supply auditing for stablecoins, and improved price-quote negotiation. Lightning Labs declared: "With this release, we are laying the foundation for trillions of dollars to flow on Bitcoin and Lightning." The ecosystem has grown to include Joltz, Speed, Lnfi Network, Amboss, and Voltage building wallets, SDKs, and tools. Tether led an $8M investment in Speed for Lightning-native stablecoin payments. Stark was a featured speaker at Proof of Talk 2025 in Paris, where she spoke on Bitcoin's future as programmable money ([Bitcoin Magazine](https://bitcoinmagazine.com/news/lightning-labs-releases-taproot-assets-v0-6-with-updates-to-stablecoin-support-on-bitcoin); [The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/307084/lightning-labs-eyes-improving-stablecoin-settlement-with-launch-of-updated-taproot-assets)).

### 2.10 Paolo Ardoino (Tether)

Paolo Ardoino (born 1984), an Italian computer scientist and billionaire, has been CEO of Tether since December 2023 and concurrently serves as CTO of Bitfinex. Under his leadership, Tether generated over **$10-15 billion in profit** in 2025 (estimates vary by source), making it one of the most profitable companies in crypto history. The company holds $186.5 billion in USDT liabilities backed by $6.3 billion in excess reserves, with up to $141 billion in U.S. Treasury exposure, $17.4 billion in gold, and $8.4 billion in Bitcoin ([CoinDesk Most Influential](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2025/12/09/most-influential-paolo-ardoino); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/01/30/tether-s-gold-holdings-top-usd17-billion-as-net-profits-surpassed-usd10-billion-for-2025); [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/01/27/tether-15-billion-profit-lceo-strong-case-finance-leaders-embrace-stablecoins/)).

In early 2026, Tether launched **USAT**, a U.S.-regulated stablecoin issued through Anchorage Digital Bank -- its first product designed to comply with the GENIUS Act (passed July 2025). USAT directly competes with Circle's USDC in the regulated U.S. market. Ardoino's diversification strategy has been aggressive: Tether invested $2 billion in energy production and Bitcoin mining (aiming to be the largest miner by end of 2025), acquired 70% of Brazilian agribusiness firm Adecoagro, paid $775 million for a 48% stake in Rumble, made a $1.29 billion offer for Juventus FC, and built a portfolio of 120+ company investments. Tether was also acknowledged by the U.S. DOJ for assisting in seizing $61 million in USDT linked to fraud. Ardoino has shifted from avoiding the U.S. to actively meeting with White House officials and collaborating with the FBI and Secret Service ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/01/why-tethers-ceo-is-everywhere-right-now/); [Fortune](https://fortune.com/article/tether-ceo-paolo-ardoino-crypto-firm-stabelcoins-howard-lutnick-usdt-cantor-fitzgerald/); [Tether](https://tether.io/news/tether-leads-8m-strategic-investment-in-speed-to-advance-lightning-native-stablecoin-powered-payments/)).

### 2.11 Changpeng Zhao (CZ)

Changpeng Zhao, founder of Binance, returned to public prominence in late 2025 after being pardoned by President Trump on October 23, 2025 -- following his guilty plea to Bank Secrecy Act violations, four-month prison sentence, and release in September 2024. The pardon was controversial: Binance spent $800,000 on clemency lobbying, and a lobbyist hired by Zhao met with Trump after being introduced by Donald Trump Jr. Democrats and some Republicans characterized it as "brazen corruption" ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng_Zhao); [CoinDesk Most Influential](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2025/12/12/most-influential-changpeng-cz-zhao)).

Post-pardon, CZ has pivoted to government advisory roles, advising approximately a dozen governments on crypto regulation and asset tokenization as of early 2026. He serves as a strategic advisor to Pakistan's Crypto Council and to the president of Kyrgyzstan on digital assets. At Davos 2026, CZ told CNBC he has "very strong feelings" that 2026 will be a "super cycle" for Bitcoin, predicting the traditional four-year boom-bust pattern "will probably break" as the U.S. goes pro-crypto. He continues to hold Bitcoin and BNB without trading. CZ confirmed Binance.US is preparing a comeback after the SEC dropped its lawsuit "with prejudice." While maintaining an estimated net worth near $80 billion on Bloomberg's list, CZ has denied this, writing "Nonsense, divide by 100 and that's about right" (~$260 million). He has launched Giggle Academy (free education platform) and YZi Labs (investment team) while clarifying he has no plans to return to Binance operationally ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/video/2026/01/23/binance-founder-changpeng-zhao-2026-will-be-a-super-cycle-for-bitcoin.html); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/01/23/binance-s-changpeng-zhao-says-bitcoin-will-break-the-four-year-cycle-this-year); [Bitget](https://www.bitget.com/academy/estimated-net-worth-changpeng-zhao-2026-comparison-crypto-leaders)).

### 2.13 Larry Fink (BlackRock)

Larry Fink, CEO of BlackRock (the world's largest asset manager with ~$11.5 trillion in AUM), has undergone what may be the most consequential philosophical reversal in the history of institutional Bitcoin adoption. The man who once dismissed Bitcoin as a "vehicle for money laundering" now describes it as "digital gold," an "asset of fear," and "not a bad asset." At the December 2025 DealBook Summit, Fink characterized Bitcoin as a hedge against currency debasement and sovereign debt, and in conversations with sovereign wealth funds, he noted Bitcoin allocation discussions now involve 2-5% portfolio positions ([CCN](https://www.ccn.com/education/crypto/blackrock-ceo-larry-fink-bitcoin-asset-of-fear/); [Bitcoin.com](https://news.bitcoin.com/blackrock-ends-2025-with-771k-bitcoin-as-ceo-larry-fink-eyes-700k-btc/)).

BlackRock's iShares Bitcoin Trust ETF (IBIT) became the fastest fund in ETF history to cross major AUM milestones, surpassing $100 billion in assets at its peak and holding approximately 786,300 BTC. Despite the early 2026 correction (Bitcoin retreating from $124K to the $60-70K range), IBIT maintained $54.12 billion in AUM as of February 10, 2026 and commands nearly 50% of all RIA-allocated crypto ETF capital. Fink envisions a path where global adoption could drive Bitcoin toward $700,000. He confirmed sovereign wealth funds have been "steadily accumulating Bitcoin, including buying as the price tumbled below $90,000." Major institutions including Harvard's endowment, pension funds, Wells Fargo (now accepting Bitcoin ETF shares as collateral), JPMorgan, and BNY Mellon have operationalized Bitcoin lending desks. By mid-2025, over 70% of institutions held digital assets, up from 40% the prior year ([TradingKey](https://www.tradingkey.com/analysis/cryptocurrencies/btc/261581598-bitcoin-btc-etf-proshares-wells-fargo-approval-odds-blackrock-ibit-gbtc-tradingkey); [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2025/12/04/sovereign-wealth-funds-were-buyers-as-bitcoin-btc-plunged-blackrock-s-larry-fink); [AInvest](https://www.ainvest.com/news/blackrock-crypto-etfs-evolution-digital-asset-institutional-adoption-2510/)).
