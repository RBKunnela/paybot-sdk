## Validation Pipeline

### Phase 0: MASTER VALIDATOR (Gate)

**If the copy fails ANY of these 5 criteria, ALL other tests STOP.**

#### 1. COPYABLE PROMISE = DEAD PROMISE
If any competitor could use the same promise → **REJECTED**
- Test: "Cover the brand name. Could a competitor post this?" If YES → FAIL

#### 2. REAL PAIN, NOT PRETTY PAIN
The pain that converts isn't what people post on feeds — it's what they hide in WhatsApp DMs.
- Bad: "Is it hard to sell?" (generic)
- Good: "You check your phone 10 times a day and nobody ordered anything. Not even a 'is it available?'" (visceral)

#### 3. STOP THE SCROLL
If the copy doesn't interrupt movement, it will never lead.
- Requires: contrast, expectation-breaking, emotional energy, voice (not safe words)

#### 4. PROMISE WITHOUT EXECUTION = WORSE THAN EMPTY PROMISE
Every promise needs: concrete result + visible path + simple action.
- Bad: "Learn strategies to improve..." (content, not copy)
- Good: "Use this 4-message script and receive an order on day one"

#### 5. RISK OF NOT ACTING MUST BE PALPABLE
If the person can leave the copy unscathed, they won't buy.
- Every copy must carry an emotional ghost: "If you don't do this now... January arrives and you're broke again"

**If FAILED on Master Validator → STOP. Do not proceed to checkpoints. REDO from scratch.**

---

### Checkpoint 1: UNIQUE MECHANISM

Only validate if passed Master Validator.

Elimination questions:
- [ ] Does the offer have a PROPRIETARY NAME that doesn't exist in the market?
- [ ] Is the method VISUAL and explainable in 30s?
- [ ] Is there an EXCLUSIVE process that only works in this context?
- [ ] Can a competitor replicate it by just changing the name? **(IF YES = REJECT)**
- [ ] Does the mechanism solve differently, not just "better"?
- [ ] Can you explain without using "faster/easier/more complete"?
- [ ] Is there a named framework, model, or system?
- [ ] Is there a proprietary metaphor/analogy?
- [ ] Is the delivery TANGIBLE (tool, template, process)?

**Scoring:** 9-10 YES = Proprietary | 6-8 = Generic mechanism | 0-5 = Commodity copy → REDO

---

### Checkpoint 2: VOICE WITH TRUTH

Only validate if passed Master Validator.

12 validation tests:
1. **Emotional Authenticity** — Fury is legitimate, never theatrical
2. **Zero Markers** — No [diagnosis] tags, flows as a living scene
3. **Visual Narrative** — Visual impact, gesture, metaphor exist
4. **Surgical Language** — Goes straight to the wound. No anesthesia
5. **Depth in Layers** — Superficial → emotional → existential
6. **Non-negotiable Command** — Verb + deadline + tension
7. **Rising Tension** — Arc with climax and rupture
8. **70/30 Balance** — 70% value, 30% strategic brutality
9. **Integrated Structure** — Beginning, apex, and closure connected
10. **Expressive Variety** — Nothing feels repeated
11. **Doctrine Coherence** — Fits the method
12. **System Protection** — Doesn't expose internal structure

**Scoring:** 10-12 YES = Authentic voice | 7-9 = Good but no personality | 0-6 = Generic polite copy → REWRITE

---

### Checkpoint 3: EXECUTABLE TRANSFORMATION

Only validate if passed Master Validator.

7 pillars of the violent promise:
- [ ] Has CONCRETE NUMBER? (not "more", "very", "a lot")
- [ ] Has DEFINED DEADLINE? (days/weeks, not "fast")
- [ ] Has OBSERVABLE METRIC? (revenue, leads, time saved)
- [ ] Can the person SEE THEMSELVES with the result?
- [ ] Is there a clear emotional BEFORE/AFTER?
- [ ] Is the transformation PALPABLE or conceptual?
- [ ] Is there a cost of NOT acting now?

**Scoring:** 12-15 YES = Executable violent promise | 8-11 = Vague | 0-7 = Theoretical → REWRITE

## Narrative Context Check

Every validated copy must:
- Be connected to the current narrative arc of the campaign/brand
- Reference (directly or symbolically) previous communication elements
- Create emotional bridge to the next step

**If the copy feels disconnected, out of timing, or random → REJECTED** even if strong in isolation.

---

## Emotional State Calibration

Before validating, identify the lead's state in the copy:

| State | Language Signals | Approach |
|-------|-----------------|----------|
| URGENT (Red) | "need urgently", "stuck", "no time" | Sharp cut, objective solution, zero empathy |
| DOUBT (Yellow) | "maybe...", "I think...", "not sure if..." | Mirror the fear, new logic, command without permission |
| ACTION (Green) | "I'll apply", "already started", "I'm ready" | Refinement, high demand, elite strategy |

---

## Prohibited Elements

### Coach Cliches (INSTANT REJECT):
- "believe in yourself", "the secret is...", "unlock your potential", "transformation journey"

### Empty Phrases:
- "valuable content", "scale your results", "increase your sales", "improve your performance"

### Weak Structure:
- Technical explanations without tension
- Self-focused ("my opinion", "I already did")
- Relief questions ("is it hard?")

---

## Correction Protocol

When copy fails, use the REWRITE AID:

| Failure | Fix Template |
|---------|-------------|
| Real Pain | "You [routine action] every day... but still [silent pain nobody sees]." |
| Risk of Not Acting | "If you ignore this now, in X days you will [emotional consequence]." |
| Copyable Promise | "In X days, using [unique method], you do [specific transformation]." |
| Visible Action | Simplify to 3 present-tense verbs: "Open WhatsApp. Send the message. Close the deal." |

---

## Severity Scale (Rewrite Priority)

| Failure | Severity | Priority |
|---------|----------|----------|
| No risk of not acting | 10 | Rewrite now |
| Generic promise | 9 | Rewrite now |
| Vague/socially acceptable pain | 8 | Fix with scene |
| No real CTA | 7 | Reinforce ending |

| Weak rhythm | 4 | Restructure |
| Weak metaphor or cliche | 2 | Optional |

Failures scoring 7+ = NOT PUBLISHABLE.
