## TL;DR

- **Crypto protocols** are the rule sets governing blockchain networks. They span Layer 0 (meta-protocols like Polkadot), Layer 1 (base chains like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana), Layer 2 (scaling solutions like Arbitrum, zkSync, Lightning), and application-layer protocols (DeFi).
- **Creating a protocol** involves consensus mechanism selection, tokenomics design, framework choice (Cosmos SDK, Substrate, EVM tooling), multi-phase auditing, and a testnet-to-mainnet launch strategy.
- **Major L1s in 2025-2026** are racing toward parallel execution and sub-second finality. Solana's Firedancer targets 1M TPS; Polkadot's JAM protocol aims to become a decentralized supercomputer; NEAR's Nightshade 3.0 separates consensus from execution.
- **Layer 2s have matured:** Base leads consumer adoption, Arbitrum leads DeFi TVL ($16.6B), ZK rollup proving times dropped from 16 minutes to 16 seconds, and Lightning Network hit all-time-high capacity.
- **DeFi** is a $238B+ market with liquid staking (Lido: $38B TVL), restaking (EigenLayer: $18B TVL), yield tokenization (Pendle: $8.3B TVL), and RWA tokenization ($33B+ on-chain, projected $100B by end of 2026).
